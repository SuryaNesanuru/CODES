Alcohol Brands, Uses, and Effects

Specially for Charan and Vishal

Types of Alcohol Brands

Whiskey

Popular Brands: Jack Daniel's, Jameson, Glenfiddich

Use: Often enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails.

Effects: Can cause relaxation, lowered inhibitions, and euphoria. Excessive consumption can lead to impaired judgment and coordination.

Vodka

Popular Brands: Smirnoff, Absolut, Grey Goose

Use: Commonly used in mixed drinks like martinis and cocktails.

Effects: Similar to other spirits, it can cause relaxation and lowered inhibitions. Overconsumption may lead to nausea and dizziness.

Beer

Popular Brands: Budweiser, Heineken, Corona

Use: Usually consumed chilled, enjoyed socially.

Effects: Can provide a mild buzz, but excessive drinking can result in hangovers and dehydration.

Wine

Popular Brands: Barefoot, Robert Mondavi, Yellow Tail

Use: Often paired with meals or enjoyed on its own.

Effects: Moderate consumption can be relaxing and enjoyable, but overindulgence can lead to headaches and impaired coordination.
